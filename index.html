About Arrowhead LLC

Arrowhead Technologies LLC (AT) is a Minority Native American owned independent testing laboratory located in Tampa Bay on the West Coast of Florida. AT was NVLAP Bulk Asbestos Fiber Analysis Lab Code 200703-0 accredited in December 2004. Our accreditation is for (PLM Test Method) EPA 600/R-93-116: Method for the Determination of Asbestos in Bulk Building Materials and EPA 600/M4-82-020: Interim Method for the Determination of Asbestos in Bulk Insulation Samples. AT is owned, managed, and directed by Monte Hall P.G. who was Lab Director for a major environmental engineering firm for more than seventeen years. Mr. Hall is a Florida licensed Professional Geologist.

AT maintains a professional liability insurance policy with coverage for over one million dollars.

Monte L. Hall, Laboratory Director
